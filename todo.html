    How to get here

    Plane ✈️ flights are about $400 round trip
    Bus 🚌 brave souls can pay $150 for a 27+ hour voyage
    Car 🚗 Albuquerque is just shy of 1,000 miles from the RGV
    Train 🚅 the BNSF is free — travel at your own risk

    Where to stay

    Wherever you stay, don't leave anything valuable in the car! 😳
    Motel 6 - $50/night
    Home2 Suites by Hilton (clean and comfy) - $130/night
    El Vado Motel (classic Route 66 vibe) - $200/night
    Hotel Albuquerque at Old Town (rustic Southwest vibes) - $243/night
    Hotel Chaco (we saw it in Better Call Saul) - $327/night

    Where to eat

    Range Cafe 🥞
    Mariscos Altamar 🦐
    El Pinto 🌮

    Tia B's La Waffleria 🧇

    What to do

    Hiking - La Luz, Pino, Volcanoes, Petroglyphs
    Indian Pueblo Cultural Center
    ABQ Botanical Gardens
    Stone Age Climbing Gym
    Visit Santa Fe (train fare is under $3 but may be $10 by August)
